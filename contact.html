Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will you respond to my inquiry? +
We respond to all email and form inquiries within 24 hours on business days. For WhatsApp messages, we typically respond within 2–3 hours during academy hours.
Can I visit the academy without an appointment? +
Yes, you are welcome to visit during our open hours (see sidebar). For a formal tour and discussion with our head coach, we recommend booking an appointment to ensure a coach is available to meet with you.
Do you offer trial lessons before committing? +
Absolutely — all new students receive a free 45-minute trial session with one of our coaches. This includes a skill assessment and introduction to our teaching methodology. No payment required.
Can I pay enrollment fees via bKash or Nagad? +
Yes. We accept bKash, Nagad, Rocket, bank transfer, and cash payments at the academy. Online payments can also be made through our student portal.
