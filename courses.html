FAQ

Common Questions

How do I know which course is right for me? +
We offer a free skill assessment — either online or in-person — to evaluate your current level and recommend the best program. Just register and we'll schedule the assessment within 48 hours.
Can I switch programs if I outgrow my current one? +
Absolutely. We encourage students to progress between levels as they improve. Your coach will recommend advancement when you're ready, and transitions happen at the start of a new module.
What is your refund policy? +
We offer a full refund if you are not satisfied within the first 7 days of joining. After that, we offer a pro-rated refund for unused sessions.
Are there any prerequisite ratings for courses? +
No FIDE rating is required for Foundations, Junior Club, or Online Classes. Tournament Preparation recommends a club-level skill base. GM Mentorship requires a FIDE rating of 1800+.
Do you offer group discounts? +
Yes — families enrolling 2 or more children receive a 10% discount. Schools and corporate groups can contact us for custom bulk pricing.
