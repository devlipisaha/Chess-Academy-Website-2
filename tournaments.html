Tournament Guidelines & FAQs

How do I register for a tournament? +
Fill out the registration form on this site or visit the academy in person. Payment can be made via bKash, Nagad, or cash at the venue. Confirmation email sent within 24 hours.
Are tournaments open to non-academy students? +
Most open tournaments welcome all players. Some internal events (like the Annual Championship) are restricted to enrolled KnightVision students.
What rating do I need to participate? +
Our open events have sections for all rating ranges including unrated players. FIDE-rated sections require an established FIDE rating. Check individual event regulations.
How are FIDE ratings calculated? +
FIDE ratings are updated after eligible rated tournaments. New players need to complete 5 rated games. Our team assists students in getting their initial FIDE rating.
