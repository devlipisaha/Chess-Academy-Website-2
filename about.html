About KnightVision

Our story, mission, values, and the passionate team behind Bangladesh's premier chess academy.

Our Story

Born from a Love of Chess

KnightVision Chess Academy was founded in 2012 by International Master Dhruv Sharma in a small classroom in Bogra. His dream was simple: to make world-class chess education accessible to every student in Bangladesh, regardless of their background or current skill level.

What started as a 12-student after-school club has grown into a full-fledged chess institution with over 1,400 enrolled students, 12 certified coaches, a comprehensive online platform, and a trophy cabinet filled with national and international honours.

Today, KnightVision is recognized by the Bangladesh Chess Federation as a model academy, and our alumni compete at national and international FIDE-rated events across South Asia.

Mission & Values

What Drives Us Every Day

🎯

Our Mission

To deliver world-class chess education that is accessible, structured, and transformative — developing not just chess skills but life skills including discipline, critical thinking, and resilience.

🔭

Our Vision

To be South Asia's leading chess academy — producing FIDE-rated players, national champions, and lifelong lovers of the game who carry the values of chess into every aspect of their lives.

💎

Our Values

Excellence in coaching. Integrity on and off the board. Inclusivity for all ages and backgrounds. Continuous improvement through rigorous self-analysis. Respect for opponents and teammates alike.
